Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
